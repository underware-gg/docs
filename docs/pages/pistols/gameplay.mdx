# Gameplay


## Challenges

A **Challenge** is a dispute between two duelists. Let's call them...

* **Duelist A** (the challenger)
* **Duelist B** (the challenged)



## Rounds

Each **Challenge** can be resolved in one or two **rounds**:

* Round 1: **Pistols**, or **paces**
* Round 2: **Blades** and other **tactics**
